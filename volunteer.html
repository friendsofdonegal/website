Volunteer Opportunities

Friends of Donegal, like other nonpprofit organizations cannot function without volunteers. Thank you to those of you who are already providing voluteer support. To others who have not yet become invoved in this effort, please consider giving back to the donegal community by volunteering for an organization whose mission is to impact the lives of our neighbors who are underresourced.
The following is a list of volunteer options (This list will change please check the website often):

    Getting ahead-weekly classes

  • Transportation to and from weekly classes
  • Childcare during weekly classes
  • Graduation coordination-occurs at the end of the 14 week class and involves planning the details of the ceremony
  • Become a facilatator-training involved 2-3 hour classes (must have bridges out of poverty training as a prerequisite

    Going ahead-monthly classes

  • Provide education-for example budgeting, credit, cooking, job interviewing

    Committees-consider joining and becoming a committee member

  • Finance/fundraising/marketing
  • Program development related for those living in poverty
  • Housing

    Miscellaneous

  • Data entry
  • Administrative tasks
