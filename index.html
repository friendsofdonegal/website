GETTING AHEAD

The Getting Ahead program is a 12 week course where individuals explore the theory of change while investigating their lives and their community. Participants develop a plan to build a path out of poverty, after exploring the mental models of economic class.

CHAMPIONS FOR TEENS

Champions for Teens is an advocate program for students in the Donegal High school who are identified as homeless.

ERAP

This program offers financial support from the federal government for those individuals who have been impacted by Covid 19 by losing a job or in a reduction in work hours since March 2020.
